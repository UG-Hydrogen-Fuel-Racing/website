MISSION OBJECTIVES

EXTRACTING COASTAL BOUNDARIES FROM SPACE
  • Leverage onboard processing to automatically extract coastal boundaries (shorelines, coastal vegetation edges) across the entire UK.
  • Compile boundary data into a timeseries to create a standardized measure of coastal change over time.
  • Equip coastal managers and decision-makers with the latest information on climate change impacts, highlighting the most affected areas.
SAVING TIME AND EFFORT
  • Coastal data collection is traditionally expensive, labor-intensive, and limited to accessible areas.
  • Satellite imagery offers new, efficient methods but still requires expert processing
  • OirthirSAT automates shoreline vector extraction, cutting lead time by 300% and reducing data package sizes.
